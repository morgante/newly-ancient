By personal, I mean that it is written solely and entirely by me, not that it concerns my life—that would be far too boring. Instead, topics discussed within these pages include design, technology, politics, and education. Newly Ancient was first <a href="http://myfla.ws/blog/2006/06/25/new-blog/">started</a> in June of 2006, when Morgante had recently completed 7th grade. In July of 2008, Morgante <a href="http://myfla.ws/blog/2008/07/19/goodbye-and-good-luck/">abandoned</a> the first incarnation of Newly Ancient to do some soul searching. On September 1 of 2008, Newly Ancient was resurrected in its present form. Format includes a mix of longer entries and shorter links meant to highlight content elsewhere. Content focuses around technology, education, and international&nbsp;experiences.</p> <h4>Who?</h4> <p>You can find out about me on <a href="http://morgante.net">my personal&nbsp;site</a>.</p>
